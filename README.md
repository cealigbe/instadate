# About instadate

**instadate** is a date idea discovery app for couples. It helps you break out of the dinner-and-a-movie routine by browsing a curated collection of date experiences, from lowkey bookstore afternoons to quirky food truck crawls, organized into categories that match your mood and energy. Instadate is an exploration into the capabilities of generative AI and AI-driven content generation.

## How it works

Land on the home page and pick a category that fits the vibe: Simple, Outdoorsy, Quick, Sophisticated, Classic, Quirky, First Date, Anniversary, or let the app surprise you entirely. From there, browse a scrollable carousel of date ideas in that category. Click into any card to view the full date suggestion, complete with a description, estimated time and cost, and a handful of tips to make the date even better.

## What's in this repo

A SvelteKit app using Svelte 5 runes. The UI is componentized around the three core views: the home category grid, the date listing carousel, and the date detail page. A shared global.css stylesheet drives the design system, with additional styling in the component files. Icons are inline Feather SVGs; typefaces are Figtree and Syne, loaded from Google Fonts.
